Our Story

Crafting Dreams Since 2025

KHUSHALI DESIGN STUDIO was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to transform ordinary spaces into extraordinary experiences. What started as a small design consultancy has grown into one of the most respected interior design studios in the region.

Our journey began when our founder, driven by an unwavering passion for aesthetics and functionality, decided to create a space where creativity meets craftsmanship. Today, we continue to push boundaries, blending traditional elegance with contemporary innovation.

Nishant Jangid

Founder & Creative Director

Our Mission

To create thoughtfully designed spaces that enhance the quality of life for our clients. We believe that great design should be accessible, sustainable, and deeply personal. Every project we undertake is an opportunity to make a positive impact on how people live, work, and interact with their environment.

Our Vision

To be the leading interior design studio recognized for innovation, excellence, and transformative design solutions. We envision a world where every space tells a story, evokes emotion, and serves its purpose beautifully. Our goal is to set new standards in design while nurturing the next generation of creative talent.
