FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical interior design project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A single room design typically takes 4-6 weeks, while a complete home renovation can take 3-6 months. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline specific to your project.

Do you work with a specific budget range?

We work with a variety of budgets and believe great design is accessible at any price point. During our consultation, we'll discuss your budget and create a plan that maximizes value while achieving your design goals. Transparency in pricing is one of our core values.

Can you work with existing furniture and decor?

Absolutely! We understand that certain pieces have sentimental or monetary value. We're skilled at incorporating your existing furniture and decor into a fresh, cohesive design. We'll assess what works and suggest strategic updates.

What's included in the initial consultation?

Our complimentary initial consultation includes a discussion of your design goals, lifestyle needs, and aesthetic preferences. We'll also tour your space (in-person or virtual), review your budget, timeline expectations, and provide an overview of our process and services.

Do you offer virtual design services?

Yes! We offer comprehensive virtual design services for clients who prefer remote collaboration or are located outside our local service area. Our virtual packages include video consultations, 3D visualizations, detailed specifications, and online shopping lists for easy implementation.

How do revisions work during the design process?

Revisions are a natural part of the design process. Our packages include a specified number of revision rounds. We present concepts, gather your feedback, and refine until you're completely satisfied. Additional revisions beyond the included rounds are available at an hourly rate.
